If you love amazing fly-fishing and beautiful scenery, Than youv'e come to the right place. Lee's Ferry is situated within a small section of the beautiful and majestic Colorado river, which meanders gracefully through the world renowned and awe inspiring Grand Canyon. Between breath-taking scenery, and phenominal Brown and Rainbow Trout fishing, "Flies on the Fly" offers guided river tours that never disapoint!
